Workforce Routing Optimization for a European Telecom Operator

In collaboration with a colleague, I developed a heuristic-based software in VB Excel to optimize auditor routes for over 2,000 stores across Spain, addressing resource constraints and minimizing costs. Our solution, leveraging a clustering phase that prioritized inter-city opportunity costs, achieved:

  • 8% overall cost reduction
  • 15% fewer auditors required
  • 10% reduction in total distance traveled
  • 6% reduction in makespan
  • 500x faster execution time compared to the original solution

Additionally, the solution improved Baobab's original approach by reducing execution time by 500x, significantly enhancing efficiency and scalability.

Development of Financial Impact Assessment Tool for Ibisa SAS

Ibisa SAS, one of Colombia's pioneering companies in providing technological solutions, focuses on evaluating clients' technological maturity, organizational structure, and core value propositions to assess the financial impact of proposed solutions.
I developed a pilot prototype software that, using a centralized database, analyzes client input to estimate the potential financial impact of implementing specific digital technologies in targeted areas of their business. The tool calculates expected revenue growth and cost reduction, providing clients with tailored insights.
As a result, the tool has:

  • Increased client engagement by 10%
  • Reduced overall service and response time, enhancing operational efficiency and client satisfaction
This solution has streamlined Ibisa's evaluation process, enabling data-driven recommendations and stronger client relationships.
